MineRival Rules

General Rules

  • Respect all players and staff.
  • No cheating, hacking, or unfair advantages.
  • No real-world trading outside of the approved shop.
  • No spamming or advertising other servers.
  • Use common sense — don’t ruin the experience for others.

Spawn Rules

  • No PvP or griefing in spawn area.
  • No traps or scams targeting new players.
  • No lag machines or bug abuse.

Lifesteal Rules

  • PvP, griefing, and raiding are allowed.
  • No duping, hacking, or exploiting.
  • No combat logging — fights must be fair.

Practice Rules

  • Follow arena guidelines.
  • No teaming in solo queues.
  • Respect cooldowns and ranked queues.
